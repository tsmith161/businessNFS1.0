Company Founding


Founded by a seasoned global risk and governance leader, NFS Insights Advisory was established to bring board-level rigor and real-world execution to organizations facing growing complexity, risk, and regulatory pressure. The firm was founded by Nancy Freda-Smith, a CPA and Certified Fraud Examiner with more than two decades of experience leading internal audit, enterprise risk management, asset protection, and governance functions for NYSE-listed and multinational companies. Drawing on a career spanning public company boards, Fortune 500 leadership roles, and advisory work at Deloitte, the firm was created to close the gap between theoretical risk frameworks and practical, value-driven solutions.


NFS Insights Advisory was built in response to a recurring challenge observed across industries: organizations often identify risks but lack the structure, discipline, or insight to act on them decisively.
